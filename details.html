What is Days Past Due and what factors affect my CIBIL Score?

DPD or Days Past Due indicates how many days a payment on that account is late that month. Your payment history is one of the factors that lenders consider when determining your creditworthiness. Making late payments or defaulting on your EMIs has a negative impact on your score.

How is DPD Calculated?

Days Past Due is determined by the disparity between the actual due date of an EMI or Credit Card bill payment and the date when the payment is actually made.
